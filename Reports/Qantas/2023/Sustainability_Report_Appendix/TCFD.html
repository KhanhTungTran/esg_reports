TCFD Overview NaN NaN NaN The Financial Stability Board (FSB) created the Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures (TCFD) to develop recommendations on the information companies should disclose related to climate change. In December 2017, Qantas Group publicly declared our support for the TCFD and its recommendations. Our response to the TCFD is included in the Climate action section of the FY23 Sustainability Report. NaN NaN NaN NaN NaN
