NaN 2.0 General Disclosures 2-19 Remuneration policies The Company distinguishes the remuneration structure of Non-Executive Directors from that of executive Directors and senior executives. The Company’s policy rewards executives with fixed, performance-based and equity-based incentives. To drive improvements in shareholder value, a significant proportion of remuneration for the CEO and senior executives is at risk’ based on the delivery of returns to shareholders. Conversely, to preserve independence and impartiality, no element of Non-Executive Director remuneration is at risk’ (i.e. it is not based on the Company's performance). Non-Executive Directors receive Board and Committee fees that are set regarding the responsibilities and risks of the role and market competitiveness.
